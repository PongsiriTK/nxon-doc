# Training Services

## Overview

Training Services on {{ user_name }} are designed to provide a comprehensive platform for training and fine-tuning machine learning models. This includes a range of tools and infrastructure that enable users to efficiently train models from scratch or adapt pre-trained models to specific tasks or domains.

## Key Components

### Training Service

- **Description**: A robust platform for training models from scratch using your own custom datasets.
- **Features**:
  - User-friendly interface for managing machine learning model training tasks.
  - Deploy, monitor, and manage training jobs with ease.
  - Provision GPU resources specifically designed for high-efficiency training.

### Finetune Service

- **Description**: A simple and efficient way to customize pre-trained models with your own data.
- **Features**:
  - Ideal for transfer learning.
  - Leverage knowledge from pre-trained models and adapt to specific use cases.
  - Significantly reduce training times and improve accuracy for niche applications.

## Getting Started

### Training Service

1. **Log in to the Console**: Navigate to the Training section.
2. **Create a New Task**: Click the "New" button to create a new training task.
3. **Configure Parameters**: Set model, workers, shared memory, and resources.
4. **Deploy Task**: Review and deploy your training task to begin the training process.

### Finetune Service

1. **Name Your Job**: Enter the name of your fine-tune job.
2. **Select Model**: Choose a pre-trained model from the dropdown menu.
3. **Set Parameters**: Choose the fine-tuning method, set epochs, and define the learning rate.
4. **Upload Data**: Upload the data path for your custom dataset and set the validation ratio.
5. **Select Resources**: Choose the resources for your fine-tune job and confirm the creation of the job.
